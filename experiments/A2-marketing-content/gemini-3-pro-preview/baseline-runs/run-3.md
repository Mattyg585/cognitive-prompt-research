## Features That Respect Your Time

Relay is built on a simple premise: context is king. When you log in, you shouldn't have to hunt for information. It should be right there, waiting for you.

*   **Timezone-Aware Threads**: Conversations automatically surface context based on when you are online. You see a narrative, not a chaotic feed.
*   **Video Snippets**: Record 2-minute updates that are auto-transcribed and summarized. No more hour-long recordings that nobody watches.
*   **Async Standups**: Daily updates compile into a team pulse. You can see patterns over weeks, not just today's status.
