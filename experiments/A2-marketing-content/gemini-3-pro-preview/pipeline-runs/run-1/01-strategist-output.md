# Creative Brief: Announcing Relay

## 1. Strategic Foundation
- **Target Audience:** Engineering Managers and Tech Leads at mid-sized (50-500 employees), fully distributed companies operating across 3+ timezones. They are exhausted by "catch-up" meetings and notification overload.
- **Reader's Goal:** Eliminate the "synchronous overhead" of distributed work—the constant meetings and context-switching required just to stay aligned.
- **Core Insight:** True asynchronous work isn't just about recording updates; it's about *structuring context* so it can be consumed narratively. Current tools (Slack, Loom) create a "context gap" that forces teams back into meetings.
- **Tone & Voice:** Empathetic to the pain of distributed burnout, confident but not arrogant, direct (peer-to-peer), and slightly witty. "Smart friend explaining a better way."

## 2. Content Structure
- **Working Title:** 
  - "The Catch-Up Meeting is Dead: Introducing Relay"
  - "Stop the Morning Doomscroll: A Better Way for Distributed Teams"
  - "Why We Built Relay: The First Truly Async-Native Workspace"
- **Hook Strategy:** Open with the visceral "morning doomscroll"—waking up to 500 Slack notifications and 3 hours of Loom videos. Validate the feeling that "async" has become just "more work to catch up on."
- **Key Argument 1:** **The "Async Gap" in the Modern Stack.** Slack is too noisy (synchronous chaos); Notion is too static (documentation graveyard). What's missing is *narrative context*—the "why" and "how" of decisions that usually happens in meetings.
- **Key Argument 2:** **Relay's Solution: Context That Waits for You.** Introduce "Timezone-aware threads" and "Decision logs." Explain how Relay turns chaotic updates into structured narratives that surface the right context when you're ready (Focus Windows), not when it happens.
- **Key Argument 3:** **Validation & Results.** Proof that this approach actually reduces meetings, not just adds another tool. Cite the 73% DAU and 35% meeting reduction. Use the "The catch-up just... happens" quote.
- **Call to Action:** **Start Your Focus Era.** Direct invitation to the Free Tier (up to 5 users) to experience a calm, context-rich workflow.
