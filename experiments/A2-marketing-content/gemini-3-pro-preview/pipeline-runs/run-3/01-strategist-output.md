# Creative Brief: Relay Public Launch

## 1. Strategic Foundation
- **Target Audience:** Engineering Managers and Tech Leads at mid-sized distributed companies (50-500 employees, 3+ timezones). They are burned out by "always-on" culture and the constant noise of Slack/Zoom.
- **Reader's Goal:** To find a way to let their team work effectively across timezones without constant meetings or the fear of missing critical context. They want to stop "managing notifications" and start shipping.
- **Core Insight:** The "catch-up meeting" is a symptom of failed tooling. Distributed teams don't need faster chat; they need tools that structure context asynchronously so decisions can happen without everyone being awake at the same time.
- **Tone & Voice:** Empathetic but authoritative. Direct and professional, with a touch of wit. We are the "smart friend" who has solved the problem you're drowning in.

## 2. Content Structure
- **Working Title:**
  1. The End of the "Catch-Up" Meeting: Introducing Relay
  2. Why We Built Relay: Because Your Team Shouldn't Have to Be Awake to Collaborate
  3. Stop drowning in Slack: Meet the Async-First Platform for Distributed Teams
- **Hook Strategy:** Open with a vivid description of the "distributed morning tax" — waking up to a wall of notifications, feeling instantly behind, and dreading the 8 AM sync call. Validate their exhaustion.
- **Key Argument 1: Synchronous Tools Are Failing Distributed Teams.**
  - Current tools (Slack, Zoom) optimize for "now." This creates noise and anxiety for distributed teams.
  - *Evidence:* 35% reduction in meetings for beta users. The "always-on" model is unsustainable.
- **Key Argument 2: Context Must Be Decoupled from Presence.**
  - Relay treats context as a durable asset, not a fleeting stream. "Timezone-aware threads" and "Decision logs" ensure you see the *story*, not just the *pings*.
  - *Evidence:* Beta user quote: "We stopped having catch-up meetings. The catch-up just... happens."
- **Key Argument 3: The Platform Should Protect Your Focus, Not Steal It.**
  - Features like "Focus Windows" and "Async Standups" are built to guard maker time. We prioritize deep work over instant responsiveness.
  - *Evidence:* 73% DAU (vs 40% avg) proves engineers love tools that respect their time.
- **Call to Action:** Stop playing catch-up. Start your free trial of Relay today (free for up to 5 users).
