Messaging Brief

- **Audience & context**: Engineering managers and tech leads at 50–500 person companies with teams spread across 3+ time zones; they need async coordination without meeting overload.
- **Core tension / job-to-be-done**: Keep distributed teams aligned on decisions and progress without drowning in notifications or scheduling more meetings.
- **Primary promise / transformation**: Relay turns scattered async updates into shared, readable narratives so alignment happens while teams sleep.
- **Proof points**:
  - 200 private beta teams
  - 73% daily active usage vs ~40% industry average
  - NPS 67
  - Reported 35% meeting reduction
  - Timezone-aware threads cited as most-loved feature in 80% of feedback
  - Beta quote: “We stopped having catch-up meetings. The catch-up just... happens.”
- **Voice / tone notes**: Direct, smart-friend clarity; confident without dunking on competitors; empathetic to notification overload; light wit when natural.
- **CTA intent**: Start free trial
- **Keywords**: async collaboration platform; distributed teams; timezone collaboration; async-first
- **Angle options**:
  - **The night-shift narrator**
    - Insight: Most async tools dump a wall of messages, not a story of what changed.
    - Framing: Relay turns overnight work into a narrative so teammates wake up aligned.
    - Proof: Timezone-aware threads and decision logs capture context, not just outcomes.
  - **Decision memory, not decision amnesia**
    - Insight: Distributed teams lose the “why” behind decisions across time zones.
    - Framing: Relay makes every decision portable by saving context and alternatives.
    - Proof: Decision logs plus thread context reduce backtracking and re-litigating.
  - **Meetings removed, clarity added**
    - Insight: Meetings are a tax on deep work; async should reduce them, not replace them.
    - Framing: Relay cuts sync time while preserving shared understanding.
    - Proof: 35% meeting reduction, 73% daily active usage, beta quote on catch-up meetings disappearing.
  - **Async-first by design, not a retrofit**
    - Insight: Retrofitted tools still center real-time behavior and notification firehoses.
    - Framing: Relay is built for asynchronous collaboration from the ground up.
    - Proof: Focus windows, narrative threads, async standups, and video snippets with auto-summaries.
  - **Team pulse over time**
    - Insight: Status updates are useful only when patterns surface, not when they’re buried.
    - Framing: Relay aggregates async standups into a visible pulse across weeks.
    - Proof: Structured async standups and trend visibility support proactive management.
