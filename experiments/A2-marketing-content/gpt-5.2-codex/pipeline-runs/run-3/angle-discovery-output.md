**Audience & context**
- Engineering managers and tech leads at 50–500 person companies with teams split across 3+ timezones
- Launching public after a private beta with 200 teams; hero blog post to anchor launch week

**Core tension / job-to-be-done**
- Keep distributed engineering teams aligned and moving without constant meetings or notification overload
- Share context and decisions across timezones so people can work independently but in sync

**Primary promise / transformation**
- Relay turns async work into a shared, timezone-aware narrative so teams stay aligned, understand decisions, and protect deep work

**Proof points**
- 200 teams in private beta; 73% daily active usage
- NPS 67; 35% reported meeting reduction
- Timezone-aware threads cited in 80% of feedback
- Customer quote: “We stopped having catch-up meetings. The catch-up just... happens.”

**Voice / tone notes**
- Direct, smart-friend voice; confident without dunking on competitors
- Empathetic to fatigue from meetings/notifications; lightly witty only when natural

**CTA intent**
- Start free trial (free tier up to 5 users; Pro $12/user/month; Enterprise custom)

**Keywords**
- async collaboration platform; distributed teams; timezone collaboration; async-first

**Angle options**
- **“The catch-up that just happens”**
  - Insight: In distributed teams, the real cost is the missing context, not the lack of updates
  - Framing: Relay auto-assembles what happened while you slept into a narrative, so you wake up aligned
  - Proof: Timezone-aware threads most-loved feature; quote about ending catch-up meetings
- **“Decisions with receipts”**
  - Insight: Distributed teams lose the why behind decisions, causing rework and misalignment
  - Framing: Decision logs capture context, alternatives, and stakeholders so anyone can trust the outcome
  - Proof: Beta adoption (73% DAU) indicates habitual use; decision logs as a differentiator
- **“Protect maker time without going dark”**
  - Insight: Constant pings destroy deep work, but silence creates drift
  - Framing: Focus windows batch notifications while keeping a clear async trail
  - Proof: 35% meeting reduction; deep work protection feature
- **“Async standups that show the real pulse”**
  - Insight: Daily standups create fragmented status; leaders need trends over time
  - Framing: Async standups compile into a living pulse, revealing patterns across weeks
  - Proof: Teams in beta using structured updates; high daily active usage
- **“Video updates that respect attention”**
  - Insight: Long recordings waste time; quick context beats hours of playback
  - Framing: Two-minute video snippets with transcripts and summaries keep updates human and skimmable
  - Proof: Built-in transcript + summary; differentiates from generic video tools
