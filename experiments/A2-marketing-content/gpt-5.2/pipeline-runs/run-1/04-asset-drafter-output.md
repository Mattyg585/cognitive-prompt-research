### Objections / FAQ
**Is this just another chat tool or an ‘AI summary’ layer?**
No. Relay is async-first: durable threads and decision context are the core. Summaries can help you onboard quickly, but the underlying context stays navigable and verifiable.

**Do we have to replace Slack/Linear/Notion overnight?**
You don’t have to. Start with one project or team where context loss is painful. Expand only if it fits your workflow.

**Are auto-summaries accurate enough to trust?**
Treat summaries as a fast on-ramp—not a source of truth. Relay keeps the thread history, decisions, and links so you can validate quickly when it matters.

**Does this turn into status theater?**
Relay is built for shared awareness, not surveillance: what changed, what’s blocked, what needs attention—without requiring everyone to be online.
