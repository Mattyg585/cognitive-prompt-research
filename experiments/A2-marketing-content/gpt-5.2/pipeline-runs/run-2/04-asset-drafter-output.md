### Problem (plain-language)
When someone’s offline, continuity breaks.

Updates splinter across chat, docs, tickets, and video—then the next day starts with:
- “What did I miss?”
- “Why did we decide that?”
- “What’s blocked, and who owns it?”

And the default answer becomes another catch‑up meeting to reconstruct context.

### What Relay does
Relay keeps a **shared, searchable storyline** of your work:
- **What changed** (the facts)
- **Why it changed** (the decision context)
- **What’s next** (the handoff)

It’s **timezone‑aware**, so the right thread of context shows up when you’re actually available.

**Isn’t this just another tool to check?**
Relay is designed to reduce tool‑hopping by turning existing work signals into one storyline of context—so you spend less time hunting and more time shipping.

No. Relay complements your stack by preserving continuity across it, without asking you to abandon the tools you already use.
