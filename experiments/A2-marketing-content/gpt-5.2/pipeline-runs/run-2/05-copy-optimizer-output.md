- “What did I miss?”
- “Why did we decide that?”
- “What’s blocked, and who owns it?”

And the default fix becomes another catch-up meeting—to reconstruct context you already created.

### What Relay does
Relay keeps a **shared, searchable storyline** of your work:
- **What changed** (the facts)
- **Why it changed** (the decision context)
- **What’s next** (the handoff)

It’s **timezone-aware**, so the right thread of context shows up when you’re actually available.
