# Pipeline Design Notes: A4 (GPT-5.3-Codex)

## Design intent

The original prompt can produce good summaries but tends to catalogue themes because investigation, synthesis, evaluation, and stakeholder framing are mixed in one context.

The pipeline targets that exact interference pattern.

## Why three stages

- **Not one stage**: keeps all contamination in context.
- **Not two stages**: synthesis and translation remain entangled.
- **Three stages** is the minimum split that isolates investigation, patterning, and strategic translation.

## Core cognitive rhythm

1. **Diverge** in Stage 1: read evidence openly.
2. **Converge** in Stage 2: construct pattern architecture.
3. **Reframe + decide** in Stage 3: translate into strategic action.

## Main expected gain

Better performance on A4’s explicit criterion:

- Baseline/optimised risk: catalogue themes.
- Pipeline target: reframe system dynamics and strategic choices.

## Trade-offs

- Higher orchestration overhead.
- Greater dependence on Stage 1 completeness.
- Lower chance of mode interference and template lock-in.

For this experiment, the trade-off is justified because quality of insight is the measured objective.
